CTC Athletics

Our Mission: CTC Athletics uses sports as a vehicle to reach the youth in our community to help them become the person they were created them to be.


Our Vision: We want to prepare athletes spiritually, physically, and academically to make their school teams and to become leaders among their peers.


Core Values:

  • Love: Unconditional love for your neighbor as yourself
  • Character: Doing the right thing all the time
  • Integrity: Honesty and strong moral principles
  • Accountability: Own up and be resposible for your actions
  • Respect: For yourself, others, the sport, and authority
  • Attitude: Competitive, strong-willed, and leadership without sacrificing moral values
  • Team: Concepts that apply to life as well as sports
  • Family Oriented: Family involvement in CTC Athletics

    • CTC Athletics Youth Programs

    The following programs provide the oppurtunity for youth in our community to develop their skills and help them achieve their goals of making their school teams:

  • Camps
  • Leagues
  • Club teams
  • Basketball
  • Track and field
  • Flag football

    • Youth Sports Camps

    Summer Youth Sports Programs for basketball and football are held during June and July. They involve both boys and girls from grades 4-6, and are instructional tools that allow skill development and technique training. They involve high school and college students as mentors and instructors.


    CTC Athletics Basketball Leagues

    Basketball Leagues include boys and girls in 4-6 grade. High school students keep score and referee while college students are used as assistant coaches and referees. It utilizes. There is competition among school teams.


  • Winter session--November through February

  • Spring session--March through May

    • Basketball Club Teams

    Basketball Club Teams are a form of competitive club basketball. It includes local travel and tournament play. Participants include boys and girls in 4-6 grade. College students will be used as assistant coaches.


  • Spring teams--March through May

  • Summer teams--June through July

    • CTC Athletics Track and Field

    Track and Field includes competition among school teams and is an instructional guide towards skill development and technique training among its participants. It allows 4-6 grade boys and girl to play and it involves high school and college students.


  • Spring session--March through May

    • CTC Athletics Flag Football

    Flag Football includes an instructional enviornment in which encourages skill development and technique training. It covers the fundamentals of football and provides competition among school teams. Its participants are boys from grades 4-6.


  • Fall session--August through October

    • Instructional Development

    This includes boys and girls in 1-3 grade. It has summer camps in June and July to teach basic skills and techniques. It also has basketball and boys flag football. It involves high school and college students as mentors and instructors.


    Impact to Our Community

    We are a positive enviornment for youth and both physcially and mentally prepare our participants to play sports for their school teams. We build character and strong values and mentor high school and college students.CTC Athletics use sports as a vehicle for the greater cause of developing strong youth who become adults giving back to the community that helped them to succeed.
